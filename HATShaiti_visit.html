    A Unique Opportunity

    HATS – Haiti encourages individuals and work teams to travel to Haiti to be a part of the work we are doing. Those who come to Haiti are given the opportunity to join in construction projects, visit in the local community, spend time with the children, and minister in church.

    You can help us in our work for the most needy and most vulnerable members of Haiti’s population.
