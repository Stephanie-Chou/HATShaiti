All donations to the Hands Across the Sea Association for the mission in Haiti are tax deductible. The board members function in a strictly volunteer capacity and we strive to keep our overhead costs low so that more of your generous donations can go to running our orphanage, school and community programs.

Meet Our Partners

HATS – Haiti is overseen in Canada by the Hands Across The Sea Association, a non-profit board of directors with members from across Canada.

HATS – Haiti is also affiliated with another charitable organization, Education Haiti in Halifax, Nova Scotia. Education Haiti’s focus is to raise funds to enable a large number of children attend school in Haiti.
